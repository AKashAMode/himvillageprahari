Ganga Bhogpur

Introduction

Our efforts in Ganga Bhogpur focus on promoting sustainable living practices and environmental awareness among the local community. From organizing clean-up drives to implementing waste management systems, our initiatives aim to preserve the natural beauty and biodiversity of the region. We work closely with the community to educate and empower individuals to take charge of their environment and build a zero-waste model for the village.
